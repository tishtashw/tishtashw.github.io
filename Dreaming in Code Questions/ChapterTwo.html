  2. What does it mean for a program to “fork?”
    • For the use of a code not really owned by anyone to split off into rivalling camps
  3. Linus Torvalds used a “science” and “witchcraft” analogy referring to software, explain.
    • When 1 person learns something new and amazing but does not share or explain it to anyone people used to explain it as witchcraft whereas something that could be explained, proven, and understood is known as science. Witchcraft has since died but science lives on strong. In Order for software development to continue on strong with new advancements it must move away from witchcraft like behaviors and begin acting more like science.
